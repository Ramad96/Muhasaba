About Qalb

Qalb (قلب) means heart in Arabic — the seat of intention, feeling, and consciousness in the Islamic tradition.

This space is a quiet corner for your daily murāqabah — a moment of gentle self-observation and remembrance. Each day a new prompt invites you to write honestly: about your intentions, your gratitude, your struggles, and your hopes.

There are no targets, no streaks, no pressure. Only you, your words, and your Lord.

"Verily, actions are by intentions." — Sahih al-Bukhari
