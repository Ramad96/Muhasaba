About Muhasabah

Muhasabah (محاسبة) is the Islamic practice of daily self-reckoning — an honest accounting of the heart between you and your Lord.

This space is a quiet corner for murāqabah — gentle self-observation and remembrance. Each day a prompt invites you to write honestly: about your intentions, your gratitude, your struggles, and your hopes.

There are no targets, no streaks, no pressure. Only you, your words, and your Lord.

"Call yourself to account before you are called to account." — Umar ibn al-Khattab (ra)
